Sales Representative – US

InnoCellence Systems is a fast-growing, innovative software development firm based in Singapore with an office / development centre in UK, Japan and China. We have several great opportunities for dynamic individuals who want to be part of our talented, multi-national team. InnoCellence offers the chance for you to work with cutting-edge technologies for some of the world’s most innovative companies.

As the company growing fast, the Sales Representative position in US is now available.

Responsibilities

  • Develop strong relationships with major customers and new business opportunities (US market as the main focus).
  • Interface with customers on their business needs and challenges, and provide/present wining proposals in collaboration with our creative team and engineering team.
  • Responsible for Account Management for US market, plan, monitor, control all aspects of the projects delivery and services to our US based customers.

Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years of experience in Sales and Marketing and 2 years senior experience in IT industry.
  • Strong strategic marketing experience in a global organisation offering niche products or services with a premium pricing strategy.
  • A proven track record of increasing sales and converting substantial new business opportunities through excellent Key Account Management and co-ordination of regional or global business development activities.
  • Knowledge and experience of working in growing hi-tech markets such as Medical, Healthcare, etc.
  • Excellent ability to influence customers and business partners on technology innovation, mobile application adoption, etc.

Please send your CV and cover letter to: careers@innocellence.com
