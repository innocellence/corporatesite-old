Inspirational corporate communication

These days, getting important news doesn’t have to be uniform, black and white and one-way. Digital means that not only can we choose the sources of our news – we can choose how we receive it. This can greatly improve our retention of crucial information. And it means important opportunities for businesses wanting better ways to communicate with their workforce.

Reach, our enterprise app, transforms corporate communication. It enables your employees to discover, watch and share information globally. Compelling multimedia and a seamless, customizable interface make receiving important company news an enjoyable experience.

And Reach doesn't just let your company quickly and easily distribute information. Sophisticated metrics enable you to see what communications are most effective in reaching and engaging your employees.

Reach provides:

  • A paperless method of distributing news to a wide internal audience
  • A common platform for users to connect and collaborate
  • Customizable user interface
  • Compatibility with range of mobile devices
  • Engaging multimedia content
  • Online and off-line access
  • Article comment and sharing functions

HTML 5 Applications

Your mobile devices let you work anywhere. You no longer need to be in the office to get the job done. But doing it well means having the right functionality – and the great user experience of HTML5.

We leverage HTML5 to free you from the office. For example with our Travel Approval Application, we took an existing PC-based travel approval system and created a better experience for the iPad – losing none of the functionality of the original software.

An HTML5-featured interface enables our enterprise partners to migrate their existing PC based systems to any mobile device, wherever they are.

QR Code for cloud-based inventory control

Managing inventories can be a complicated and time-consuming business. Traditional systems - using manual data entry - not only take away crucial production time: They're unreliable too.

Our mobile solution saves time and delivers 100% reliability. An iOS app scans QR codes with all the necessary item information, for easy record-keeping and stock management. Meanwhile, notifications send the accumulated information to a cloud-based system. And because all this is automated, it frees the workforce to focus on production.

Ideal International Meeting Planner

If you are like us, and have friends and colleagues around the world, you often need to schedule meetings and appointments between global locations and time zones.

Our app makes it much easier to secure important time with your colleagues and customers. By enabling you to see times in other countries, you can quickly find a meeting slot that works for everyone. Email and calendar sharing features let you get organized easily. This handy app saves you time and ensures you will never miss an important meeting again.
